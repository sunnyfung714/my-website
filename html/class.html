Class




The range of group fitness classes provided by ML Strength is listed below.




BODYPUMP: Strength. A rapid fat-burning class using weights to completely tone and condition the body. Increased bone density and good posture will also be a side effect!


BODYSTEP: Cardio/Strength. A high energy cardio blast whilst toning your hips, butt and thighs.


BODYCOMBAT: Cardio/Strength. Martial art techniques are modified and delivered in this hi-energy calorie burning class. Seriously fun, seriously motivating!




BODYVIVE: Strength/Cardio. Low impact and strength band moves designed to increase posture and functionality for daily life.


AQUA FIT & AQUA MODE: Cardio/Strength. Burn calories in the pool! Use water as resistance to get results with little or no impact to your body and joints.


BOXING & TAE BOX: Cardio/Strength. Get fit fast with upper body combinations and cardio bursts to create definition and burn calories…fast! …And don’t forget you can book a sparring session in our boxing ring.




STEP MOVES, STEP BASIC & STEP BURNER: Cardio/Strength. Your Instructor will choreograph high energy step routines to tone your hips, butt and thighs.


SCULPT & COMBO: Cardio/Strength. Your instructor will choreograph both cardio and strength routines to burn fat and tone your body!




POWER HOUR: Cardio/Strength. Your instructor will choreograph a freestyle circuit combining cardio and strength moves to burn fat and tone your body!


ABT: Cardio/Strength. Your instructor will choreograph a routine to focus on your Abs, Butt and Thighs! The name says it all!


ABS BLAST: Strength. Your instructor will choreograph a 30min class focusing on your abs, front, back and sides!




BODYATTACK: Cardio. A high-energy, calorie-consuming athletic workout with strong, simple moves that will get you results fast!


ZUMBA: Cardio. Zumba fuses funky Latin rhythms and easy-to-follow moves. If you love to dance this class is for you!


RPM: Cardio. Get on your bike with this ultimate calorie burner! The terrain is set so you can really challenge yourself. The biggest calorie burner!




BODYJAM: Cardio. Be ready to have a lot of fun with this pre-choreographed dance routine. Get the party started!


CYCLE: Cardio. Get on your bike with this ultimate calorie burner! Your instructor will choreograph the terrain so be prepared! The biggest calorie burner!


CYCLE EXTREME: Cardio. Get on your bike for longer with this ultimate calorie burner! Your instructor will choreograph the terrain so be prepared for this extended 60min ride.




REJUVENATE, LOW IMPACT & GENTLE FITNESS: Low Impact Cardio. Your instructor will choreograph basic combinations to maintain general fitness for daily activity and well-being.


CARDIO BLITZ & CARDIO CIRCUIT: Cardio. Your instructor will choreograph Med-High impact aerobic routines to challenge you and increase your cardiovascular fitness.


WORLD DANCE & DANCE: Cardio. Freestyle dance at its best and dependent on what our instructor’s specialty is! Please ask at your club.




HILO & FAT BURNER: Cardio. Your instructor will choreograph cardio aerobic routines designed to keep your heart rate in your fat burning zone!


BODYBALANCE: Flexibility. A combination of Yoga, Pilates & Tai Chi helps you achieve a balanced life both Mind and Body.


PILATES: Flexibility/Strength. Develop balanced, long, lean muscles on the outside and strong muscles on the inside whilst improving your posture.




YOGA: Flexibility. A complete mind/body workout to de-stress, increase flexibility and build natural strength.


TAI CHI: Flexibility. Traditional Tai Chi to rejuvenate mind, body and soul.


CHI BALL: Flexibility. Traditional Tai Chi using a small exercise ball to rejuvenate mind, body and soul.
